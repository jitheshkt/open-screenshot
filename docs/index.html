Everything you need, nothing you don't

The features that matter, done well — without the bloat and permissions the others ship.

Three capture modes

Visible area, full scrolling page (stitched), or drag out any region — from the popup or a keyboard shortcut.

Gradient backgrounds

Linear or radial gradients at any angle, a preset palette, plus padding, rounded corners and shadow.

Annotate

Arrows, lines, circles, text, highlights and blur/redact. Select, move and delete — right on the canvas.

Copy or download

Export a pixel-perfect PNG or copy straight to your clipboard. What you see is exactly what you get.

Private by design

No account, no analytics, no network calls. Only activeTab access — no broad-host warning.

Truly open source

MIT licensed. Read every line, audit the permissions, request features, or fork it and make it yours.

Capture → beautify → share

Three steps, a few seconds.

Capture

Click the toolbar icon or hit a shortcut, then pick visible area, full page, or a region.

Beautify

Add a gradient, padding and shadow, then annotate with arrows, text, highlights and blur.

Share

Copy to clipboard or download the PNG. Your style settings are remembered for next time.

🔒 Your screenshots stay yours

Open Screenshot does everything locally in your browser. It collects no personal data, has no analytics, and makes no network requests. Read the full policy for the details.
